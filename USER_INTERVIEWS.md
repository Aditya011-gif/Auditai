# User Interviews

> [!CAUTION]
> Do not fabricate this file. The Credex assignment explicitly states that fake interviews are an instant disqualification. The template below must be filled with real conversations before submission. Three interviews are the minimum. Each should be 10–15 minutes with a startup founder, engineering manager, or ops lead who currently pays for at least two AI subscriptions or API accounts.

## What to Ask

1. Walk me through how you decide which AI tools your team uses.
2. How often do you review your AI spend? What triggers a review?
3. What is your current monthly total across all AI tools and APIs? (rough estimate is fine)
4. Have you ever downgraded, consolidated, or cancelled an AI subscription? What happened?
5. Who approves new AI tool purchases at your company?
6. If you ran a free audit right now and it found $200/mo in waste, what would you do next?
7. What would make you trust or distrust the savings numbers?
