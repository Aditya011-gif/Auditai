## FAQ

**Is this connected to my billing account?**  
No. The MVP uses self-reported spend so users can get value without OAuth or procurement friction.

**Does AI calculate the savings?**  
No. Deterministic rules calculate savings. AI only writes a short summary.

**When does Credex appear?**  
Credex is surfaced prominently for reports with more than `$500/mo` savings or high API-credit opportunities.

**Is the public report private?**  
The public report strips email, company, role, and other lead fields.

**What if my stack is already efficient?**  
The report says so honestly and offers pricing-change alerts instead of manufacturing waste.
